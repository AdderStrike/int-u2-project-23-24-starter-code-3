How to have Consistency and why the

world needs it

Why do we need consistency?

We need consistency everyday to keep the world running! Without consistency, the world would be a place where nothing big got done.

Why do some people not want consistency?

Consistency has been shouted down for years in favor on instant gratification. While it's better to have patience in things, instant gratification is satisfying for right now.

How do I get more consistent?

  • Respect
  • Patience with others
  • Patiece with yourself
  • Persistence
